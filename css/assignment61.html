Toulouse, France

In the south of France, Toulouse, may not be the first vacation destination you think of. But with its rich age old history and entertainment facilities you would be surprised by the endless posibilities. One of the many historically rich sites is the Vomitorium palace where gladiators fought in the time of the Romans. Besides that you have several culturally rich museums in the area. Toulouse also has a spaceflight centered discovery center with 4 million visitors to date. More info

Paphos, Cyprus

Paphos is a culturally rich city in cyprus with a legend saying it is the birth place of Aphrodite the goddes of love. Besides the legends there are several archaeological sites to explore during your trip. Aphrodites brewery company is an example of the locals embracing the legends around its city. An archaeological site worth visiting is the Kato Paphos archaeological site. Besides the obvious tourist spots you also have amazing resturaunts and sighseeing spots all around the city. More info
